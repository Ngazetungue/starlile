Management

The Board

Our board consists of experienced professionals dedicated to the success and growth of African Stars Football Club. They provide strategic direction and oversight, ensuring that the club continues to thrive both on and off the field.

The Club

African Stars Football Club is managed by a skilled team of executives and staff who handle the day-to-day operations. From marketing and finance to player development and community outreach, our company is dedicated to maintaining excellence in all aspects of the club.

AFRICAN STARS FOOTBALL CLUB

With 7 Namibia Premier League titles (1994, 2008-09, 2009-10, 2014-15, 2017-18, 2022-23, and 2023-24) and 5 Namibian Cup titles (2007, 2010, 2013, 2014, 2018) under our belt, African Stars Football Club has established itself as one of the top teams in the league. We have also won the Standard Bank Cup once in 2015 and were runners-up once in 2014. Additionally, we finished runners-up in the Dr. Hage Geingob Cup in 2014 and 2015 and placed third in 2016.

African Stars Football Club has also made several appearances in CAF competitions, including 2 appearances in the CAF Champions League (2019 and 2020), 1 appearance in the CAF Confederation Cup (2014), and 1 appearance in the CAF Cup (1992).

African Stars Football Club is a proud and accomplished football club that has significantly impacted the Namibian football scene. We continue to strive for excellence on and off the field and have a bright future ahead.
