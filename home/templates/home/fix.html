AFRICAN STARS FOOTBALL CLUB

African Stars Football Club is a professional Namibian football club based in Windhoek, Namibia. We play in the country's highest division, the Namibia Premier League, and have made a name for ourselves with our outstanding achievements.

African Stars Football Club is a proud and accomplished football club that has significantly impacted the Namibian football scene. We continue to strive for excellence on and off the field and have a bright future ahead.
